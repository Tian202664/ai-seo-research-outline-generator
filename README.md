# AI SEO Research & Outline Generator

An AI-powered SEO workflow that transforms keywords, competitor insights, and market research into strategic content opportunities and optimized article outlines.

## Overview

This workflow automates SEO content planning by combining:

- Keyword analysis
- Competitor research
- Topic opportunity discovery
- AI content strategy
- SEO outline generation


## Workflow Architecture

User Input
↓
SEO Research Agent
↓
Topic Strategy Agent
↓
Topic Selection
↓
SEO Outline Agent


## Inputs

- Target keywords
- Topic direction
- Competitor articles
- Additional requirements


## Outputs

- SEO research report
- Content opportunities
- Recommended topics
- Selected topic strategy
- SEO optimized article outline

- Dify Workflow
- Large Language Models
- SEO Research Methodology
